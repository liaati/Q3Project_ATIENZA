Fire Prevention



With the summer season already coming through, along with it is Fire Prevention Month which is every March. As we know, schools have held routinely fire drills in case of emergencies. But what about at home? It is important to know how to prevent a fire and what to do when one does start. Fires can occur anywhere, at the most unexpected times, so it is better to be informed, alerted, and ready.
